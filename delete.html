Delete Account & Data

You can delete your Delta account and all associated data at any time directly in the app. Follow the steps below or contact us via email.

📱
Delete your account in the app
1
Open the Delta App and sign in.
2
Tap the Profile tab in the bottom right corner.
3
Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".
4
Confirm the deletion. All your data will be permanently deleted immediately.
⚠️
This action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently and irreversibly deleted.
🗑️
What gets deleted?
Google account link (name, email address)
All tasks and completed items
All habits and streaks
All calendar entries and time blocks
XP, level and earned badges
All statistics and progress data
Delta Pro subscription status
📧
Request deletion via email
If you no longer have access to the app, you can request deletion of your data by email. Please include the email address you used to sign up for Delta. We will process your request within 30 days.
